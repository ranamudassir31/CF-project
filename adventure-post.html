The Grand Canyon, USA

Famous for hiking, whitewater rafting, and rock climbing, the Grand Canyon offers adventure seekers a breathtaking landscape to explore.

The Himalayas, Nepal/India/Tibet

The Himalayas are a mecca for trekkers and mountaineers, with iconic adventures like the Everest Base Camp Trek and Annapurna Circuit.

Costa Rica

From zip-lining through rainforests to surfing and wildlife watching, Costa Rica is an adventure lover's paradise with diverse landscapes.

Iceland

Explore glaciers, volcanoes, and geysers, or take a thrilling snowmobile ride across Vatnajökull Glacier in Iceland's dramatic landscape.

New Zealand

The adventure capital of the world, New Zealand offers bungee jumping, skydiving, and world-class hiking trails in Queenstown and beyond.

Patagonia, Argentina/Chile

Known for its rugged mountains, glaciers, and fjords, Patagonia offers some of the best trekking and outdoor adventures in the world.

Swiss Alps, Switzerland

From skiing to paragliding, the Swiss Alps offer adventures in some of the most stunning mountain landscapes on Earth.

Africa’s Serengeti, Tanzania

Go on a thrilling safari to witness the Big Five and experience the Great Migration in the heart of Tanzania's Serengeti National Park.

Borneo, Malaysia/Indonesia

Climb Mount Kinabalu, explore rainforests, and encounter unique wildlife like orangutans in Borneo’s diverse ecosystems.

Queen Charlotte Track, New Zealand

A scenic hiking trail that offers breathtaking views of the Marlborough Sounds, perfect for outdoor adventurers.
