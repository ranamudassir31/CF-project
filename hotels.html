Capella Bangkok

A luxury 5-star hotel on the Chao Phraya Riverfront in the Charoenkrung district, Bangkok, Thailand.

  • Rooms: 101 rooms and villas with floor-to-ceiling windows, balconies, and terraces.
  • Amenities: Swimming pool, spa, fitness center, and a greenhouse garden.
  • Dining: Michelin-starred fine dining, teahouse, and cocktail bar.
  • Family-Friendly: Children under 11 stay free when sharing a parent's bed.
  • Pet-Friendly: One small dog allowed per room (up to 10 kg).

"Picturesque, welcoming, and luxurious with spacious rooms and attentive service."

Cheval Blanc

Located in some of the world's most iconic destinations, Cheval Blanc offers an unparalleled experience of art de vivre.

  • Rooms: Elegantly designed rooms and suites with bespoke interiors and breathtaking views.
  • Amenities: Exclusive spas, infinity pools, private beach access, and state-of-the-art fitness centers.
  • Dining: Gastronomic dining led by world-renowned chefs with a focus on French and local cuisine.
  • Services: Personalized butler services, curated excursions, and unique cultural programs.
  • Family-Friendly: Dedicated activities and experiences for children and families.

"A haven of sophistication and serenity, offering guests a truly unforgettable escape."

Passalacqua, Lake Como

A luxurious boutique hotel situated on the shores of the breathtaking Lake Como, offering an exquisite blend of history and elegance.

  • Rooms: 24 uniquely designed suites with stunning views of Lake Como and lush gardens.
  • Amenities: Private gardens, lakeside infinity pool, tennis court, and an exclusive spa.
  • Dining: Fine Italian cuisine with locally sourced ingredients, served in a romantic lakeside setting.
  • History: Once home to noble families and artists, preserving the charm of 18th-century Italian architecture.
  • Experiences: Private boat tours, cooking classes, and cultural excursions around Lake Como.

"An intimate haven of tranquility, perfect for romantic escapes and unforgettable celebrations."

Aman Tokyo

A serene urban sanctuary blending contemporary luxury with traditional Japanese design in the heart of Tokyo.

  • Location: Occupies the top six floors of the Otemachi Tower, offering panoramic views of the Imperial Palace Gardens and Mount Fuji.
  • Rooms: Spacious suites with floor-to-ceiling windows, traditional Japanese accents, and luxury amenities.
  • Amenities: Spa, 30-meter swimming pool, fitness center, and exclusive Aman Lounge.
  • Dining: Gourmet Japanese and international cuisine, served with an emphasis on fresh seasonal ingredients.
  • Experiences: Tea ceremonies, wellness programs, and cultural tours exploring Tokyo's rich heritage.

"A harmonious blend of minimalism and indulgence, offering a peaceful retreat above the bustling city."
