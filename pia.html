About PIA

Pakistan International Airlines (PIA) is the national flag carrier of Pakistan. Known for its extensive domestic and international network, PIA connects major cities within Pakistan and numerous global destinations. Established in 1946, PIA is recognized for its affordable fares and dependable service.

Why Choose PIA?

  • Wide domestic and international route coverage.
  • Affordable and flexible flight options.
  • Customer-friendly service and reliable schedules.
